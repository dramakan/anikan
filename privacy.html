Privacy Policy & Legal Terms

Last Updated: May 2026

Data Collection & Usage

At Anykan, your privacy is a top priority. We collect minimal personal information strictly necessary to provide and improve our services.

Account Information

When you register, we collect your email address, username, and encrypted password. This is securely managed via Google Firebase Authentication.

User Preferences

We store your "My List" saves, continue-watching history, and profile avatars in our database to sync your experience across devices.

VIP Subscriptions, Ads, and Limitations

We offer premium subscription tiers (Elite VIP and Crown VIP) to enhance your viewing experience. By purchasing a subscription, you agree to the following terms, features, and technical limitations:

Elite VIP Benefits

Removes all website UI ads (popups and banners specifically generated by Anykan pages), unlocks 1080p quality, and allows 2 shared profiles.

Crown VIP Benefits

Includes all Elite benefits, expands to 4 shared profiles, and actively neutralizes hidden popups inside the video player using our advanced embedded click-shield technology.

The Embed Limitation (Ad Disclaimer)

Anykan does not host video files. We utilize third-party embed links (such as Vidsrc, 2embed, etc.). While our Crown VIP tier heavily suppresses popup layers over the player, some embedded hardcoded ads or highly resilient popups may occasionally bypass our filters. Because we do not control the servers providing these video streams, we cannot guarantee a 100% ad-free video player experience in every scenario.

Refund Policy

Due to the manual verification process and the immediate access granted to digital streaming benefits, all VIP subscription purchases are final, non-refundable, and non-transferable under any circumstances.

Platform Protections & Legal Disclaimers

By using Anykan, you acknowledge and agree to the following legal protections concerning content hosting and liability:

Zero Hosting Policy

Anykan operates strictly as a web directory and indexing tool. We do not host, store, or upload any video, media, or copyright-protected content on our servers. All media is streamed directly from unaffiliated third-party services.

DMCA & Copyright Infringement

We deeply respect intellectual property rights. However, since we do not host any of the video files, copyright infringement claims and takedown requests must be directed directly to the third-party video hosts (e.g., Vidsrc, 2embed, etc.). Cookies & Tracking

We use local storage and essential cookies to ensure the website functions correctly.

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in securely.
  • Preferences: To remember your selected theme (Dark/Light) and volume settings.
  • Watch History: To save your progress locally if you watch without an account.

We do not use intrusive tracking cookies for personalized external advertising.
